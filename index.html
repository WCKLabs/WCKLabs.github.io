What WCK Lab Does

WCK Lab develops and pilots lightweight digital tools that support operational coordination during emergencies and periods of disruption.

These tools are designed for use by frontline teams and partners to:

  • Share time-sensitive operational information
  • Support coordination across distributed teams
  • Reduce communication breakdowns during fast-moving events

WCK Lab projects are deployed in limited, real-world contexts and refined based on operational needs.

Services

WCK Lab provides:

  • Operational coordination tools
  • Transactional messaging for field operations
  • Program and deployment notifications
  • Limited pilots with approved partners

All services are used for operational and coordination purposes only.

SMS Program Information

WCK Lab uses SMS messaging strictly for operational and transactional communications.

If you opt in, you may receive text messages related to:

  • Operational updates
  • Coordination or deployment information
  • Program-related notifications
