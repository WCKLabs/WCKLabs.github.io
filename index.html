Innovation Kitchen alerts

Innovation Kitchen is a World Central Kitchen pilot. We use SMS, WhatsApp, and RCS messages to coordinate test and live meal support activations with chefs and partners.

By signing up for Innovation Kitchen alerts, you agree to receive SMS, WhatsApp, and RCS messages about Innovation Kitchen test and live activations. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies by activation. Reply HELP for help and STOP to cancel at any time. Signing up is not required to receive meals or support from World Central Kitchen.
